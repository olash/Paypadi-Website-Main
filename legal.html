Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At Paypadi, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our campus mobility payment app.

1. Data Collection

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide you with secure and instant ride payments. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Your name, phone number, email address, and campus ID (to verify your status as a student or staff).
  • Transaction Data: Details of payments made through the Paypadi platform, including amounts, timestamps, and the driver's unique ID.

2. Location Data & Tracking

We do not track your location in the background.

Unlike traditional ride-hailing apps, Paypadi is a payment layer designed for campus mobility. The passenger app does not require continuous background GPS tracking to process rides. Transactions are securely initiated by scanning the driver's unique QR code or manually entering their Cab ID, actively minimizing the location data collected from passengers.

3. Third-Party Processors

We do not sell your data. We share information only with trusted third parties necessary to process your transactions securely. Notably, all card payments and wallet funding are securely processed by Paystack, a PCI-DSS certified payment gateway. Paypadi does not store your full card details on our servers.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data and transaction history only for as long as your account is active, or as required by Nigerian financial regulations. If you choose to delete your account, your data will be permanently erased in accordance with our Account Deletion policy.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@paypadi.com.

Terms of Service

Last Updated: April 2026

Welcome to Paypadi. By accessing or using our application, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service.

1. The Paypadi Service

Paypadi provides a digital payment layer that facilitates instant transactions between passengers (students/staff) and independent campus transport unions or drivers. We provide the wallet, the QR scanning technology, and the payment infrastructure.

2. Payment Terms & Fees

To keep the platform operational, fast, and secure, Paypadi charges a transparent ₦5 convenience fee per successful ride payment. For example, if your transport fare is ₦200, exactly ₦205 will be deducted from your wallet. There are no hidden charges, maintenance fees, or surprise deductions.

3. User Conduct

You agree to use Paypadi strictly for lawful purposes. You must not:

  • Attempt to defraud drivers or the platform using fake transaction receipts.
  • Reverse or chargeback wallet-funding transactions after funds have been spent.
  • Share your PIN or account credentials with third parties.

4. Limitation of Liability

Important Notice: Paypadi is exclusively a financial technology payment layer, not a transport provider.

We do not own the vehicles, nor do we employ the drivers. We partner with existing campus transport unions. Consequently, Paypadi is not liable for the condition of the vehicles, the conduct of the drivers, delays, accidents, or lost items during your commute. Your transportation agreement is strictly between you and the driver/union.

5. Termination

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate your account immediately if we detect fraudulent activity, abuse of the platform, or violation of these Terms.
